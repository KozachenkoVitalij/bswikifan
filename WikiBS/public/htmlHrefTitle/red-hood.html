Red Hood

“...Grimm. It's me. Open your eyes.”

--Red Hood

 Red Hood is a recurring character in the Black Souls series. She is one of the main supporting characters in the game. To make Red Hood appear in a cycle, one must approach the stairs north of the bonfire in Lutwidge Town from above while being at 0 or less SEN and progression 1. Approaching the stairs for the first time at positive SEN and progression 0 will make a Red Beast appear instead. Doing so or reaching higher progression will make it impossible to meet Red Hood in that cycle.

Location

Red Hood can be found in different locations through Wonderland. She will only appear if the player has 0 or less SEN and will disappear from an area if a certain condition is met during the cycle. Talking to her in 10 different locations is required to access the Little Girl's Nightmare and some special interactions. All the encounters listed below count. If not mentioned differently, she will appear next to the bonefire of the area.
  • Lutwidge Town: approach the stairs north of the bonefire coming from the Head Hunting Beast boss room while at 0 or less SEN. Must be the first encounter.
  • Rabbit Hole: will disappear if progression is 3 or above.
  • Liddell Cemetery: will disappear if the progression is 3 or above.
  • Ripon Cathedral: will disappear if the lever in the basement is pulled.
  • Garden of Hearts: will disappear if Red Castle Frissel is reached.

After meeting Red Hood 10 times, one can find her again in Lutwidge Town south-east of the Kidnapper. Defeat the Beggar Wolf to complete the encounter.

Also appears in the Little Girl's Nightmare.
